About Nancy Buchko

Nancy Buchko's law office is located at 5120-50 Street in Tofield, Alberta. For easy reference, our office is on the west side of the south end of the town's main street, across from the Alberta Treasury Branches.

Tofield is located east of Edmonton on Highway 14. It is north of Camrose and is easily accessed via Secondary Highway 833 to Highway 14. It is approximately a 40-minute drive from either the south end of Edmonton or Camrose.

Nancy Buchko has been practising full-time in Tofield since 1985 and has been a member of the Law Society of Alberta since 1984.

Professional Credentials

With nearly four decades of experience serving the Tofield community and surrounding areas, Nancy Buchko brings extensive knowledge and dedication to her legal practice. She is committed to providing professional and personalized legal services to her clients.
