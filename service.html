Notary & Commissioning Services

Notary and commissioning services are available. Appointments for these services must be made in advance.

We do not provide notary and commissioning services on a walk-in basis. Please plan ahead and contact our office to schedule an appointment. This applies to all requests, including:

  • Personal Guarantees
  • Certified True Copies
  • Declarations
  • Travel Letters
  • Powers of Attorney
  • Other commissioning requests

How to Request Notary Services

To arrange for notary or commissioning services, please contact our office by phone or email. Have your documents ready and provide details about your needs so we can schedule an appropriate appointment time.

If you require notary services urgently, please contact us directly to discuss your timeline and see if we can accommodate your request.
